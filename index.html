About Us

Who we are -

Kisan Mitra is startup with the vision to strengthen fruits and vegetables trade in India. We supply fruits and vegetables to Major directly from producers to consumers which will supress the middle man loses. We want to tied up all the farmers from whom we procure fruits and vegetables and supply it to our customers through technology.

Kisan Mitra connects Indian farmers directly to businesses across the country by taking care of the complete India supply chain, from the farm directly to the buyer’s doorstep.Kisan mitra, with aim to connect farmers directly with Customers so that Farmers will get best returns & customers will get quality products with feasible rates.
